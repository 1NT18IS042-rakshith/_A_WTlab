mustang

The Ford Mustang is a series of American automobiles manufactured by Ford. In continuous production since 1964, the Mustang is currently the longest-produced Ford car nameplate. ... From 1965 to 1973, the Mustang was derived from the 1960 Ford Falcon compact..
